Overnight Oats


This is a recipe for overnight oats. I love overnight oats.
Ingredient List:
  • banana
  • oats
  • almond butter
  • chia seeds
  • almond milk
  • maple syrup
  • vanilla extract
  • salt

Steps:
  1. PLace sliced banana in the bottom of jar and layer almond butter, chia seeds, and oats
  2. mix almond milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract and pour over oats
  3. cover and refrigerate overnight
