Lasagna

This is a recipe for lasagna. I love lasagna. Lasagna lasagna lasagna
Ingredient List:
  • ground beef
  • onion
  • tomato
  • italian seasoning
  • salt and pepper
  • cheese
  • egg
  • lasagna noodles

Steps:
  1. brown meat with onions
  2. mix cheese and egg
  3. preheat oven to 370 degrees
  4. layer all ingredients
  5. bake at 375 degrees
